A Little Bit About Me...

Originally from the Midwest, I've recently made my home in South Carolina with my husband, two kids and two dogs. My journey into tech started with the opening of my first business as a massage therapist. I needed a website for my practice and after completing some free online courses, the site went live and I was hooked. Wanting to learn more I signed up for a web developer course at Skillcrush, where I learned how to design and code a portfolio site from scratch. Why yes, I built the very site you've landed on. Creating my website, I have found the freedom & passion I am looking for in a career.
